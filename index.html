What is ShikshaSaathi?

The Challenge in Rural Education

Government schools in India, especially in rural areas, face deep-rooted challenges like teacher shortages, learning gaps from rote memorization, and a growing digital divide. Millions of children are denied access to personalized, modern education, impacting their future and India's growth.

Without early exposure to tools like AI, these students risk being left behind in the future workforce.

Our Solution: An AI-Powered Companion

ShikshaSaathi is a GenAI-powered teaching assistant designed for the smart TVs, tablets, and mobile devices already being deployed in government schools. It acts as a 24/7 AI teacher that students can interact with through voice or text in their own language.

Upcoming Features: The Future of Learning

Photo-Based Q&A & Language Expansion

Upload a photo of a textbook page or blackboard, and our AI will explain it. We are also expanding to more regional languages like Telugu, Bengali, and Tamil.

Adaptive Learning Paths

ShikshaSaathi will track student progress and tailor explanations and quizzes to their individual learning speed, ensuring every student builds a strong foundation.

Empathy Coach & Offline Mode

Our AI will learn to detect frustration in a student's voice and suggest a new way to learn. An offline mode will also allow learning even without an internet connection.

Real-World Integration

Take a photo of any object in the real world, like a plant or an experiment, and our AI will build an instant, interactive lesson around it.

Collaborative Learning

Empowering teachers and students to contribute questions and answers to a shared, AI-moderated knowledge base, creating a collaborative learning community.

Advanced Gamification

Engage students with leaderboards, progress dashboards, and team-based challenges to make learning a fun and rewarding experience for everyone.
